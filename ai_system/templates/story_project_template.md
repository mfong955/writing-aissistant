# Fiction Project Template

## Suggested Directory Structure

### Core Directories
- `characters/` - Character profiles and development
- `settings/` - Locations, world-building, environments
- `plot/` - Story structure, scenes, chapters, outline
- `research/` - Background information, references, fact-checking
- `drafts/` - Writing drafts and versions
- `notes/` - General notes, ideas, and brainstorming

### Character Profile Template

```markdown
# [Character Name]

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: [Legal name, nicknames]
- **Age**: [Current age]
- **Role**: [Protagonist/Antagonist/Supporting]
- **Occupation**: [What they do]

## Physical Description
- **Appearance**: [Height, build, coloring]
- **Distinguishing Features**: [Scars, tattoos, unique traits]
- **Style**: [How they dress, present themselves]
- **Mannerisms**: [Gestures, habits, quirks]

## Personality
- **Core Traits**: [3-5 defining characteristics]
- **Strengths**: [What they're good at]
- **Flaws**: [Weaknesses and blind spots]
- **Motivations**: [What drives them]
- **Fears**: [What they avoid or dread]
- **Values**: [What matters most to them]

## Background
- **History**: [Key life events that shaped them]
- **Family**: [Family relationships and dynamics]
- **Education**: [Formal and informal learning]
- **Formative Experience**: [Event that made them who they are]

## Relationships
- **[Character Name]**: [Nature of relationship]
- **[Character Name]**: [Nature of relationship]
- **Conflicts**: [Who they clash with and why]
- **Allies**: [Who supports them]

## Voice & Dialogue
- **Speech Patterns**: [How they talk]
- **Vocabulary**: [Word choices, education level]
- **Catchphrases**: [Repeated expressions]
- **Internal Voice**: [How they think]

## Story Arc
- **Starting Point**: [Who they are at story beginning]
- **Character Goal**: [What they want to achieve]
- **Internal Conflict**: [Personal struggle]
```
